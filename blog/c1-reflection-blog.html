Cultural Amnesia

Seeking mindfulness

My EDA Experience

My take is that I am so grateful to have brilliant hard working coaches like Shereef, who believe in us & who will guide us on our journey. The founders and coaches have identified a real need, developing a unique learning opportunity for those who dare!

My Impressions

My impression right now is - I am thinking this is actually happening, I am really here working with a fabulous team of people. I tell myself to open my eyes and ears. Impressed into me now is Shereef's comments re 'working socially'. I took from that web developing or coding is a people business. We need to interact with colleagues, team members, coaches, clients & users where-ever they be. To make the code real, meaningful & be mindful of others.

How Will I Engage?

I am going to engage in this culture by being prepared to rethink some of my processes I currently use. I will be authentic but accept that I will be changing some of my own behaviors, attitudes & actions. I like the following quote and apologize for not knowing the author.

Everyone wants to change the world but nobody wants to change themselves.

My Expectations

The EDA has far exceeded my expectations.My belief is the EDA is cultivating a culture & a set of values that work, that I respect & want to be part of it. I expect to 'trip up' and make mistakes along the way - I already have in the last few days. Have asked for help & received it graciously - I hope. I want to be available if anyone calls on me for my help. I am prepared to be humbled but within the knowledge that I am with the best bunch of people to help me along my journey. At times I may falter so I hope to receive a gentle prod in the right direction.

Am I Excited? Am I Nervous?

I told myself before starting Phase-0 (and will probably remind myself often) go in with an open mind & heart. Focus on the now! And Louise don't forget to breathe. That is the nervs. Yes I am nervous - but it is excited, eager, wide-eyed anticipation nervousness that I come with. I have been handed this wonderful opportunity & it will be a privilege to be part of the EDA environment.
