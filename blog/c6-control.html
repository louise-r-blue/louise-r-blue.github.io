Learnings from Chapter 5

Since we pair programmed this week on a quote-generator app I will sum Chapter 5 learnings in quotes. "If you want to change anything in your life, change the channel & change the frequency by changing your thoughts"-unknown & "I am the master of my thoughts"-unknown

2 Minutes of Mindfulness

My 2 minutes of mindfulness occurred around the time of pair-programming. I found the practice lead to better productiveness both physically & emotionally.

Pair Programming & Emotional Intelligence Skills

This was the first time I had experienced working with a fellow developer on-line. Having never met each other in person there was initial trepidation but I quickly put that aside. I imagined that my pair colleague also had this feeling & was able to let trepidation go. My main feeling was not really about getting the code right or working but really one of - not wanting to let my pair colleague down. I do not believe this is a bad or negative feeling but rather an incentive feeling to respond to what was required. I enjoyed the experience & felt I pulled on my emotional intelligence skills when needed in a positive manner & with the right attitude.
