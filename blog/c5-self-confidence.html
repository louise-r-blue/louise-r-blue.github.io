The Relationship Between Mindfulness and Self-awareness

Both mindfulness and self-awareness have similar traits.

Self-awareness is the (trainable) ability - enabling us to look at ourselves objectively, for example during an emotional confict - how do we deal with our emotional responses to this event.

Mindfulness also practices this, but I think with a more purposeful and meaningful approach. The mindful person is attentive and focused on one particular moment or event without being rash or too quick too judge.

Self Awareness Practice

I then practiced the 2 minutes of journalling - choosing 'What am I feeling now is..'. I am not surprised I managed to write so much. I feel much is going on that is reflected in my feelings about the 'much' that is going on. Now that sounds like a bad case of recursion. My Emotions are not Me

My emotions are changeable and define a small part of the inner at some point, they do not define the physical me.

What emotions I may have had in the pass have no bearing on my emotions now or in the future.
